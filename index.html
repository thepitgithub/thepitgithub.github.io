Frequently Asked Questions

What the hell is this?

The worlds first 1 on 1 video roast battle app. Open the app, hit the Go button, meet a random stranger, start roasting.

What is roasting?

Roasting someone means that you know how to be tactfully mean (snarky) without being mean-spirited. Watch this video for inspiration.

How does it work?

You get matched with random strangers for 2 minutes at a time. Each time you get matched you get two 30-second turns to roast the other person. When it's done you match with someone new. You can keep your roasts private or make them public for the world to see and vote on. Winners get prizes.

What am I supposed to roast them about?

Every user has a profile so you can get to know your opponent before you get in The Pit.

Why is it called The Pit?

Because that's where you go to get roasted. In The Pit.

Does this cost money?

Nope.

Who else is using this?

A friend of a friend told us Kevin Hart jumps in the pit.

Do I have to use my name?

Only if you want to.

What if my feelings get hurt?

Just brush it off and jump back in. Part of the fun is learning how to take it.

What can I say??

Anything you want. But don't be mean. Being mean kills the community.

Do I need to be on camera?

Yeah, you need to have skin in the game. We're building a community here.

Are there group battles??

Not right away but we're working on it.

Are roasts public?

Nope.
